allCHEMIE by Kedrigern

This program is for manipulating with Czech chemistry terminology (transform to natural language and other way). Thus is only in Czech (include notes, docs etc.) and translate to English is useless.

Ukázka, převodu CO2, H2SO4 na slovní vyjádření:

vstup(c,1,o,2).
oxid uhlik-icity vstup(h,2,s,1,o,4). kyselina sira-ova
